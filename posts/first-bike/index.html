Bike Rap

Jan. 15, 2023

Been ridin’ my Yamaha for six years strong
Got it when I was young, but now I’m grown
I was excited about my job, wanted to indulge
Bought a bike when I could’ve walked, that’s the truth I won’t dodge

It’s reliable daily commute, and weekend fun
From city streets to winding roads, it’s my number one

Yamaha FZ, from 2017, my ride or die
From a young buck to a grown guy
Style, confidence, and independence
A coming of age, a life experience

It’s more than just a bike, it’s a state of mind
Meditation and focus, physical and mental grind
Accomplishment with every kilometer
Feels like a sense of pride
Memories to last a lifetime, an experience of a kind
Though I drive the wheels these days, I can’t help but rewind

Endless riding, nostalgia divine, cannot deny,
Always by my side, my Yamaha Ride
