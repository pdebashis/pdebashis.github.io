Yamaha FZ-S 2016

Nov. 29, 2024

Owning a bike was a dream during college which became a reality within a year of leaving college. This bike was purchased on 16 Jan in 2017 and sold on 11 Nov 2024. The machine never dissapointed but when it was time to let it go, I let it go. While selling the bike, it had 30363 km on the odometer. Here is the odometer timeline and following that is a short chatGPT generated ode to the bike.
