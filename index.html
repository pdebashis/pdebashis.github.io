First Over the Board Chess Tournament

The DreamHack Rapid and Blitz was organized by NODWIN Gaming, Samay Raina and ChessBase India. It was an open to all tournament with a prize fund of Rs.10 lakh and an entry fee of Rs.1299. I participated in the rapid section which was held on 4th and 5th of Novemeber, 2022 in the Hitex Exhibition Centre in Hyderabad. Tournament Details - https://chess-results.com/tnr684721.aspx The rapid event had a time control of 15 mins + 10 seconds of increment and 9 rounds (4 rounds on day 1 and 5 rounds on day 2).
