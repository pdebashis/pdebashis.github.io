Monitoring Hadoop

A Hadoop ecosystem is a network of computers pooling its resources to solve larger problems. The ecosystem mainly consists of a filesystem and multiple individual components which sits atop the filesystem. Such an ecosystem definitely requires vigorous monitoring to identify any abnormality and prevent major downtimes. Major Hadoop service providers, provide their own tools for monitoring. But for a standalone hadoop, the monitoring aspect is an open field, and you can use your own tools and software.
